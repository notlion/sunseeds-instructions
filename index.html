Sun Seeds are iridescent, geometric, hanging sculptures. Made from a dichroic material, they change color with the angle of the light and cast beautiful rainbow shadows and reflections.

They were originally created as an outdoor art installation by Ryan Alexander, Monique DeSalvo, and Toby Schachman. In October 2019, we ran a crowdfunding campaign to produce kits for people to make their own Sun Seeds.
